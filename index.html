Welcome to Evergreen Academy

Founded in 2025 by Kenny Akeem, Evergreen Academy has been providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. We believe in holistic development that balances academic rigor with extracurricular activities.

Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive community where students can discover their passions, develop character, and achieve their full potential.
